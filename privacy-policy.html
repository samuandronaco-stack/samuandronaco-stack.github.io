Content DRAFT Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Content DRAFT (the "App") handles information when you authorize it to access your TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API.

1. Information We Access

Content DRAFT accesses the following data through TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting API:

  • Basic account information (user.info.basic): used solely to authenticate the authorized user.
  • Content publishing permissions (video.publish): used to upload photo carousels to your TikTok account on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

Data accessed through the TikTok API is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account.
  • Upload photo carousel content that you have explicitly prepared and approved.

We do not sell, share, or use your data for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on the authorized user's device and are never transmitted to third-party servers. No personal data is stored remotely.

4. Third-Party Services

Content DRAFT uses the following third-party services:

  • TikTok API — for content publishing. Subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy.
  • Google Drive API — for reading source images. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — for temporary image hosting during upload. Images are deleted immediately after the upload completes.

5. Data Retention

No user data is retained beyond the duration of an active session. Temporary image files hosted on GitHub Pages are deleted automatically after each upload.
